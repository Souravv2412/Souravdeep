About Me

Built for operations. Wired for organization.

I am Souravdeep Singh, a student leader and operations-focused professional based in Windsor, Ontario. I hold a Post-Graduate Diploma in Data Analytics for Business from St. Clair College (GPA 3.5) and a Bachelor of Business Administration from SPPU Pune University (CGPA 8.0, Distinction).

Most recently I served as Vice President (2025-2026) of the Student Representative Council at St. Clair College Downtown, where I represented over 10,000 students, managed event budgets, coordinated cross-departmental operations, and attended provincial student advocacy conferences in Toronto. Before that I worked as a Receptionist and Education Counselor at EEC Global in India, where I managed front-desk operations and guided clients through international study pathways.

The four projects in this portfolio were built to demonstrate real operational engineering skills: document governance, scheduling architecture, financial reporting, and email operations. Every file is real and downloadable.
